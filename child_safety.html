Child Safety Standards

Last updated: 20 July 2026

U Say I Listen is operated by Nexonect Technologies OPC Private Limited. This page sets out our standards against child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE), as required by Google Play's Child Safety Standards policy.

We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation. Any account found to be involved in CSAE is terminated immediately and permanently, and reported to the relevant authorities.

1. The service is for adults only

  • U Say I Listen is restricted to users aged 18 and over.
  • Users confirm they are 18 or older when creating an account.
  • The app is rated and distributed for adults only on Google Play.
  • Where we identify an account belonging to a person under 18, we terminate it and delete the associated data.

2. Prohibited conduct

The following are strictly prohibited and result in immediate, permanent termination:

  • Any sexual conduct, content, or conversation involving a minor
  • Grooming, solicitation, or attempted contact with a minor for sexual purposes
  • Sharing, requesting, or describing child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Sexualising a minor in any form, including in speech, profile text, or images
  • Using the service to arrange contact with a minor
  • Misrepresenting your age in order to access the service

These rules are also set out in our Terms of Service.

3. How to report a child safety concern

By email, at any time

Email nexonect@gmail.com with the subject line Child safety report.

Please include, where you can:

  • The display name of the account concerned
  • The approximate date and time of the call or interaction
  • A description of what happened

Reports are treated as urgent and reviewed as a priority.

You do not need an account to submit a report, and reports may be made anonymously.

4. How we respond

  1. Triage. Child safety reports are prioritised above all other reports and reviewed as soon as they are received.
  2. Immediate suspension. Where a report is credible, the account is suspended pending review, preventing further contact with other users.
  3. Investigation. We review account records, call metadata, and any other available evidence. We do not record call audio, so investigation relies on reports, account data, and metadata.
  4. Termination. Confirmed violations result in permanent termination. Any pending earnings are withheld.
  5. Reporting to authorities. We report confirmed CSAE to the appropriate authorities in India, including the National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal, and cooperate fully with law enforcement requests.

5. Preventive measures

  • Accounts require a verified email address before any call can be made or received
  • The service is 18+ only and is not marketed to or directed at children
  • Accounts terminated for child safety violations are permanently barred from re-registering
  • We retain call metadata for 24 months, which supports investigation of reported incidents

7. Compliance

We comply with applicable child safety laws in India, including the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act, 2012 and the Information Technology Act, 2000 and rules made under it. We also comply with Google Play's Child Safety Standards policy and with the CSAE prevention requirements that apply to social applications.

8. Reporting to authorities in India

If you believe a child is in immediate danger, contact your local police, or:

  • National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal - cybercrime.gov.in
  • Childline India - 1098
  • Police emergency - 112
